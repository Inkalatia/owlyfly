关于 OwlyFlyAbout OwlyFly

OwlyFly是一款专为ESL/EFL课堂使用而设计的教育游戏，适用于各种触摸屏设备，包括智能屏幕、Android平板电脑和Windows系统。OwlyFly is an educational game designed for ESL/EFL classroom use on various touchscreen devices, including smart screens, Android tablets, and Windows systems.

该游戏通过引人入胜的游戏机制帮助学生培养英语技能、批判性思维和解决问题的能力，教师可以轻松将其融入课程计划。学生还可以在家中继续学习，巩固课堂知识。The game helps students develop English skills, critical thinking, and problem-solving abilities through engaging gameplay that teachers can easily incorporate into lesson plans. Students can continue learning at home to reinforce classroom knowledge.
